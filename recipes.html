Power Protein Bites

One of the best gains and healthy bites recommended for athletes.

ingredients

  • 1 scoop whey protein (flavor of choice)
  • 1 scoop creatine monohydrate
  • 1 scoop collagen peptides
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond butter (or peanut butter)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut (optional)
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons water (if needed for consistency)

Instructions:

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, whey protein, creatine monohydrate, and collagen peptides. Stir well to ensure even distribution of the supplements.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Add almond butter, honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract to the dry mixture. Stir until it forms a sticky dough. If the mixture is too dry, add water a tablespoon at a time until you get the desired consistency.
  3. Add Mix-ins: Fold in the shredded coconut and dark chocolate chips for added flavor and texture, if desired.
  4. Shape the Bites: Roll the mixture into small bite-sized balls using your hands. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Chill: Place the Power Protein Bites in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to firm up.
  6. Store and Enjoy: Once firm, you can store them in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week. Perfect for a quick snack before or after a workout!
